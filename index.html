Gen Li

 

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Statistics at the Chinese University of Hong Kong.

Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Statistics and Data Science at the Wharton School at University of Pennsylvania, co-advised by Professor Yuxin Chen and Professor Yuting Wei. I received my Ph.D. in the Department of Electronic Engineering at Tsinghua University, advised by Professor Yuantao Gu. Before my Ph.D., I received my bachelor's degree from the Department of Electronic Engineering and Department of Mathematics at Tsinghua University in 2016.

I am interested in diffusion based generative model, reinforcement learning, high-dimensional statistics, machine learning, signal processing, and mathematical optimization.
