Gen Li

 

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Statistics and Data Science at the Wharton School at University of Pennsylvania, co-advised by Professor Yuxin Chen and Professor Yuting Wei. Previously, I received my Ph.D. in the Department of Electronic Engineering at Tsinghua University, advised by Professor Yuantao Gu. Before my Ph.D., I received my bachelor's degree from the Department of Electronic Engineering and Department of Mathematics at Tsinghua University in 2016.

I am interested in reinforcement learning, high-dimensional statistics, mathematical optimization and machine learning.
